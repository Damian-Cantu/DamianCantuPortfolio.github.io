Sandia National Labs
Internship

I had the privilege of interning and conducting research at Sandia National Laboratories through the Data Mine at Purdue University from Fall 2022 to Spring 2023.
During this enriching experience, my team and I developed a poster for our final presentation at the Purdue Corporate Partners Symposium, showcasing the insights and outcomes of our project.

During my research with Sandia Flight and my experience with the Data Mine at Purdue, I deepened my understanding of data science and programming. At the Data Mine, I honed my skills in SQL and Python, focusing on data cleaning and managing large datasets.

As a member of the "Flight" team at Sandia Flight, I applied these skills to a challenging project: reassembling fragmented flight paths using the provided datasets. Our team dedicated significant effort to cleaning and organizing the data while developing a machine learning algorithm capable of dynamically stitching the fragmented flight paths together. This hands-on experience not only enhanced my technical abilities but also taught me the value of collaboration in solving complex problems.
